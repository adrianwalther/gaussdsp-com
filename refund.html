Email us at hello@gaussdsp.com with your order number and the email address used at checkout. We will process your refund within 3–5 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

Upon issuing a refund, your license key will be deactivated and you must uninstall the software. Continued use of the software after a refund has been issued is a violation of our Terms of Service.

If you initiate a chargeback without first contacting us, we reserve the right to permanently ban your account from future purchases. Please reach out — we are happy to make things right.
